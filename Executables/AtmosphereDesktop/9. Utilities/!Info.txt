A short description of the utilities.
HiBit Uninstaller: a powerful program for uninstalling programs with advanced cleanup and other useful cleanup tools
WizTree: see a visual graph of whats taking space on your drive
WizFile: really fast file search
CrystalDiskInfo: gives status info about your hdd/ssd
PowerToys: Microsofts' made utilities like text extractor, power rename and command palette
7zip: archive type (.7z and .zip) file manager
